Purpose

Anvato had game-changing video streaming and storage technoglogy, but their app wasn't designed for tablets or mobile devices

Anvato's product makes it easy for media companies to organize their video content, prepare it for distribution in different markets, and release it online. Their clients wanted to be able to use the application on-the-go, so I was brought on to create new responsive designs for the site.
